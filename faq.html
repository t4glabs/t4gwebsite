Is the Fairfield Programming Association a non-profit?

Yes! We formed the FPA as a non-profit in the beautiful state of Connecticut and then earned our 501(c)(3) designation from the IRS a few months later. Because of our status, we can accept donations and hopefully avoid conflicts of interest in financing.
